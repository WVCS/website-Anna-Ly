    CAS Networks: Our Mission

    Founded in 2050, we have been the #1 most trusted company to provide services outside Earth. Our intent is to provide the ability to connect yourself to the world outside the limits of reality and immerse yourself in experiences that last a lifetime. We hope that you enjoy your stay in the code worlds, either through residence, business or travel while being assured of its security and safety.
