    Our origins

    Since 2050, we have provided transport to millions of citizens daily, providing connection, accessibility and security.

    Our company started as a research company to create a system that can relieve Earth's overpopulation and our diminishing resources. After the first successful code worlds in the 40s, we were able to connect those worlds with our original world, creating the first network. We have updated the worlds and expanded them, testing their limits and their utility in providing services. Advanced technology has helped us to develop our transportation methods and world capabilities, allowing us to have the system today.

    100 years and counting, we still hope to expand and create even more. We continue to address the problems of overpopulation and hope to provide everyone satifactory experiences.

    The process

    Our worlds are created through the work of coders, artists and product managers

    1. Our skilled artists have created the realistic graphics that encompasses the worlds. Each world is different to the different styles through through planning and research.

    2. These graphics are incorporated into the world UI. Our talented coders work hard to determine any bugs and develop the code worlds that millions live in today.

    3. These products go to the Product Managers that provide the services to the population.

    Each process are deeply valued and are what makes our company special.
