    • The Project

    This website is based on my characters and the story I am currently writing and developing. It involves a company that creates virtual worlds made of code, where people can move between them. Because of overpopulation, many people choose to stay within these “code worlds,” creating this system, which is updated and expanded on over time by CAS Networks (the company)

    Here are some of my inspirations to help visualize:
  • The internet
  • Online chat games
  • IP addresses (for code world stations)
  • Packets (passports)

    • Although the website isn’t fully complete due to my inexperience, I hope to improve the web design further over time!
